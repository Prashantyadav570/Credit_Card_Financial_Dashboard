# Credit_Card_Financial_Dashboard
# 💳 Credit Card Financial Analysis Dashboard | Excel & Power BI 

## 📌 Project Summary

This project is an end-to-end **Business Intelligence Dashboard** developed using **Exce** and  **Power BI** to analyze credit card customer behavior and financial performance.

The dashboard converts raw transaction and customer data into meaningful business insights that help management monitor revenue, understand customer spending patterns, identify profitable customer segments, and support strategic business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Financial institutions generate millions of credit card transactions every month. However, without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to answer questions such as:

* Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
* Which credit card category is most profitable?
* Which states contribute the highest business?
* Which age group spends the most?
* Are transactions increasing or decreasing over time?
* Which payment methods are preferred by customers?
* How much interest income is generated?
* Which customer groups should be targeted for marketing campaigns?

This dashboard provides answers to these business questions through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

* Analyze customer demographics and spending behavior.

# 📊 Dashboard Overview

The project consists of two interactive dashboards.

## 1️⃣ Customer Dashboard

Provides customer-centric business insights including:

* Total Revenue
* Interest Earned
* Customer Income
* Customer Satisfaction Score
* Revenue by Gender
* Revenue by Age Group
* Revenue by Education
* Revenue by Occupation
* Revenue by Marital Status
* Revenue by State

---

## 2️⃣ Transaction Dashboard

Provides transaction-level analysis including:

* Total Transaction Amount
* Total Transaction Count
* Revenue Trend
* Revenue by Quarter
* Revenue by Card Category
* Revenue by Transaction Type
* Revenue by Payment Method
* Weekly Revenue Analysis
